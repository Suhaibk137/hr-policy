3. Compensation and Benefits

3.1 Salary Structure

3.1.1 Salary Payment Procedures

At Elite Resumes, we have established a structured pay scale system to ensure fair and competitive compensation for all employees. Our pay scales are designed based on the following criteria:

  • Job Roles and Responsibilities: Compensation is aligned with the specific duties, responsibilities, and impact of each role within the organization.
  • Experience and Qualifications: Salaries are determined by the level of experience, educational qualifications, and relevant skills that an employee brings to their position.
  • Market Standards: We regularly benchmark our pay scales against industry standards to remain competitive and attract top talent.
  • Performance Levels: Compensation may vary based on individual performance, with higher achievers receiving greater rewards.

3.1.2 Salary Reviews and Adjustments

To ensure that our compensation remains competitive and equitable, Elite Resumes conducts regular salary reviews and adjustments:

  • Annual Reviews: Salaries are reviewed annually to account for inflation, market trends, and individual performance.
  • Performance-Based Increases: Employees demonstrating exceptional performance, taking on additional responsibilities, or contributing significantly to company goals may be eligible for salary increases.
  • Promotions: When employees are promoted to higher roles, their compensation is adjusted to reflect the increased responsibilities and expectations.
  • Cost of Living Adjustments (COLA): Periodic adjustments may be made to account for changes in the cost of living, ensuring that employee salaries maintain their purchasing power.

3.2 Salary Payment Procedures

3.2.1 Payment Schedule

Regular Full-Time Employees:

  • Salary Components: Monthly compensation includes a fixed salary and applicable incentives based on performance and role-specific metrics.
  • Payment Date: Salaries are credited on the 5th of every month. If the 5th falls on a holiday, the salary will be credited on the next working day.
  • Calculation Period: Salaries are calculated from the 1st of the month to the end of the particular month.

Contractors:

  • Payment Terms: Compensation for contractors is determined based on the specific work agreements and deliverables.
  • Payment Schedule: Payments are processed as per the terms outlined in the contractor agreements, which may vary depending on project milestones or deliverable submissions.

3.2.2 Salary Calculation

  • Fixed Salary: A predetermined amount agreed upon during the hiring process, reflecting the employee’s role and experience.
  • Incentives: Performance-based incentives that reward employees for meeting or exceeding their targets and contributing to the company’s success.
  • Deductions: Applicable deductions as per local laws and company policies, including taxes, provident fund contributions, and other statutory deductions.
